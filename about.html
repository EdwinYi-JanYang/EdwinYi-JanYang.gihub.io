EDWIN YI-JAN YANG

Thanks for visiting my website

My name is Edwin Yi-Jan Yang, and I am a third year student at the University of Waterloo.
I believe one of the truest joys in life is learning about the ingenious ways things work, and then being able to apply that knowledge to benefit society.
I love all sorts of things, including computer science, space, new groundbreaking technologies, and even graphic design and psychology.
All of humanities knowledge is at our fingertips, and meeting passionate people and talking about their experiences is something I enjoy.
I love working with a team to hash out difficult problems, I find it very rewarding.
I am very motivated, and I am not afraid to fail on my way to accomplishing my academic, social, and physical goals.
Further, people who know me well describe me as being an individual sensitive to the needs and welfare of others,
because in building each other up we can achieve things one person simply cannot.
